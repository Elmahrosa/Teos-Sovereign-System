# Compliance & Safeguards Framework

TEOS is designed to operate within existing legal and regulatory frameworks.

## Regulatory Compatibility
- Supports jurisdiction-specific policy controls
- Enables KYC/AML alignment where required
- Facilitates reporting and supervisory access

## Audit & Accountability
- Immutable logs for identity attestations, governance actions, and financial events
- Evidence generation for independent audits
- Role-based oversight access

## Data Protection & Privacy
- Selective disclosure patterns for identity credentials
- Permissions and access controls by role
- Alignment with local data protection requirements

## Procurement & Institutional Controls
- Non-disruptive integration posture
- Clear separation of responsibilities
- Controlled pilots with defined scope and metrics
