# Terminology & Institutional Standards

## Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)
Foundational systems enabling services at population scale (identity, payments, data exchange).

## Institutional Readiness
Compatibility with legal, regulatory, procurement, audit, and oversight requirements.

## Safeguards
Controls ensuring transparency, accountability, privacy, and responsible use.

## Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)
Results-based measurement of outputs, outcomes, and impact (SDG-aligned where relevant).
