# Ministerial Impact Map (Illustrative)

This mapping is illustrative and must be tailored to each institution's mandate and constraints.

## Finance / Treasury
- Modules: T-Finance, T-Audit, T-API
- Impact: improved reporting, reduced leakage risk, audit-ready oversight
- SDGs: 1, 8, 16

## Planning / Economic Development
- Modules: T-API, T-Audit, T-FDI
- Impact: program monitoring, results reporting, investment transparency
- SDGs: 9, 17

## Education
- Modules: T-ID, T-Audit
- Impact: verifiable credentials, reduced fraud, portability
- SDGs: 4

## Health
- Modules: T-ID, T-API
- Impact: credentialing, access integrity, secure verification
- SDGs: 3

## Transport / Logistics
- Modules: T-Infra, T-RWA, T-Audit
- Impact: supply chain transparency, port/logistics oversight
- SDGs: 9, 11
